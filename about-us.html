about us

DU Eyewear is headquartered in Marietta, GA Family-owned and operated for over 12 years specializing in the manufacturing of eyewear, sunglasses, readers, and cases.

Filling the skies with waterfowl today, tomorrow and forever.

DU Eyewear has donated over 1 million dollars to help save waterfowl habitats and support hundreds of conservation projects to date. 

With each frame we donate a percentage of the sale to Ducks Unlimited. To date they have saved over 14 millions acres of wetlands. To find more information on Ducks Unlimited conversation effects please visit Ducks.org.

See the outdoors,
protect your eyes,
conserve the wetlands.

Designed with the adventurous in mind, DU eyewear is created to protect your eyes while enjoying the outdoors. Whether you are in blind hunting, or hiking or boating this product is perfect for the outdoors men.

We believe in saving our wetlands for future generations. Every purchase of DU eyewear will benefit conservation efforts by Ducks Unlimited.

To date Ducks Unlimited has saved over 14 million acres of wetlands in North America. We are proud to be a part of this with over a 1 million in donations.
